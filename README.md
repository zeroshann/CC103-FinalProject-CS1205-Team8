## рҹ“– About the Project

Fast food chains face significant challenges managing orders efficiently вҖ” especially during **peak hours**. Problems like inaccurate orders, delays in handling urgent requests, and long wait times lead to **customer dissatisfaction** and reduced service quality (Blankenship, 2025).

**QuickServe** is a *C++ console-based application* designed to solve these problems by helping restaurant staff manage orders in an organized, efficient, and prioritized manner.
